In our daily life, we often encounter repetitive clicking tasks, such as downloading a small batch of files on a certain webpage, cleaning up various social software information, and some ticket-grabbing activities. Our current information technology has developed to an unprecedented height, and AI has already achieved a certain level of intelligence. Therefore, I think there should be a software to assist us in these repetitive clicking operations. 

So I developed a small software to solve the problem of repetitive clicking. Based on the functions of this software, I named it "Assist of Hand".
